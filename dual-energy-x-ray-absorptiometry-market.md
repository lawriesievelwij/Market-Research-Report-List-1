<p><p>The Dual-energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) market is segmented into two primary types: Axial Bone Densitometers and Peripheral Bone Densitometers. Axial Bone Densitometers measure bone density primarily in the central areas like the hip and spine, providing comprehensive assessments for osteoporosis diagnosis. In contrast, Peripheral Bone Densitometers assess bone density in extremities such as the wrist and heel, offering portable options for initial screenings or risk assessments. Both play vital roles in evaluating bone health and fracture risk.</p></p>
