<p><p>A compression plate is a medical device used in orthopedic surgeries to stabilize fractured bones by distributing stress across the fracture site. These plates allow for better alignment and healing of fractures, making them essential in procedures involving long bone repair. Various materials, including stainless steel and titanium, are commonly used to manufacture compression plates, ensuring durability and biocompatibility.</p><p>The Compression Plate Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an increasing global population, a rise in sports injuries, and advancements in orthopedic surgical techniques. Additionally, the aging population is more prone to bone fractures, further boosting demand for compression plates. Innovations in manufacturing processes and materials are leading to the development of lighter and more durable plates, enhancing surgical outcomes and patient satisfaction.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards minimally invasive surgical techniques, which has created a demand for specialized compression plates that cater to such procedures.
