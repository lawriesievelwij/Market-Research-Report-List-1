<p><p>The automotive defogger glass market comprises three main types: windscreen, backlite, and sidelite. Windscreen defoggers are designed for front visibility, ensuring clear vision in adverse weather. Backlite defoggers focus on the rear window, enhancing safety during driving by preventing fog buildup. Sidelite defoggers are integrated into side windows, improving visibility for lateral maneuvers. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining clear visibility, thereby enhancing overall driving safety and comfort in varying climate conditions.</p></p>
