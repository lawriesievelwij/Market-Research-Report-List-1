<p><strong>The Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valves are critical components in various industries for regulating fluid flow. Gate valves provide on-off control with minimal pressure drop, making them ideal for isolation. Control valves adjust flow rates based on system demands, ensuring optimal operation. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with a quarter-turn mechanism, providing tight sealing. Butterfly valves, characterized by their rotating disc design, enable efficient flow management and space saving. Additionally, other valve types, such as check and globe valves, cater to specific applications across different sectors.</p></p>
