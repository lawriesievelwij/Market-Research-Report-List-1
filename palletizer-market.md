<p><p>The palletizer market comprises two primary types: traditional palletizers and robotic palletizers. Traditional palletizers use mechanical systems for high-speed stacking, ideal for consistent product shapes and sizes, mainly in manufacturing environments. In contrast, robotic palletizers leverage advanced robotics to handle diverse products, offering flexibility and adaptability in layouts. While traditional systems excel in speed and volume, robotic options provide versatility in handling various shapes and sizes, catering to the evolving demands of modern packaging and logistics.</p></p>
