<p><p>The polyurethane (PU) dispersions market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based PU dispersions are eco-friendly, offering lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and a safer alternative for coatings, adhesives, and textiles. Conversely, solvent-based PU dispersions provide enhanced durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications, though they may contain higher VOC levels. Both types serve diverse applications across automotive, construction, and consumer goods, catering to different performance and environmental requirements.</p></p>
